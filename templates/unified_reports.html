Safety limits for radiation workers

Alerts are generated automatically according to the following limits:

Hourly limits:
  • ✅ Safe: ≤ 0.3 μSv/h
  • ⚠️ Warning: > 2.38 μSv/h
  • 🚨 Danger: ≥ 2.38 μSv/h
Daily limits:
  • Daily limit: 54.8 μSv/day
  • ⚠️ Warning at: 43.8 μSv (80%)
  • 📊 Monitor at: 27.4 μSv (50%)
Annual/weekly:
  • Annual limit: 20 mSv/year
  • Weekly limit: 383.6 μSv/week
  • Natural background: ~0.3 μSv/h
Note: This system is designed for trained radiation workers in line with ICRP‑103 and IAEA guidelines.
